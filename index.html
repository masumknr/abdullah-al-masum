About Me

I am an Electronics and Embedded Systems Engineer with expertise in circuit design, analysis, troubleshooting, and embedded system development. Proficient in microcontrollers like Arduino, ESP32, and NodeMCU, I excel in programming with C and C++.

Skilled in PCB design, including schematics, library creation, and layout optimization, I use tools like Altium, EasyEDA, Proteus, and PlatformIO to deliver efficient solutions. Passionate about innovation, I am committed to creating impactful, real-world engineeringÂ solutions.

Services

PCB Design

  • ECAD tools: Altium Designer, EAGLE, KiCad.
  • Schematic capture, library creation, and maintenance.
  • Complex, mixed-signal, multi-layer PCBs (12+ layers, HDI).
  • BGA designs (down to 0.5mm pitch).
  • High-speed, sensitive, and high I/O-count designs (RF, FPGA, DDR3, PCIe, USB3).
  • Design for EMC and signal integrity.
  • Mechanical considerations, shielding, connector I/O layout.
  • Stack-up and build-up design, controlled impedance.
  • Production file generation and ordering.
  • Component sourcing, communication with fab and assembly houses.

Circuit Design

  • System-level design and requirements capture.
  • Part selection.
  • Analogue (power, audio, RF) and digital (MCU, FPGAs/SoCs, ..) circuit design.
  • Simulation.
  • In-house tests (full-equipped electronic lab available).

Embedded Firmware

  • Pin-out planning for MCUs, FPGAs, and SoCs.
  • Driver generation for peripherals.
  • Board bring-up.
  • Languages: C/C++, VHDL.
